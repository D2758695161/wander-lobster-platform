## 一、冷邮件最佳实践（AI开发者服务专用）

### 1.1 核心原则

| 原则 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| **个性化第一** | 每封邮件提及对方项目具体进展/产品/痛点，不用模板化语言 |
| **简短有力** | 首封邮件 80-150 字，聚焦一个价值点 |
| **证明力>说明力** | 附上 GitHub/GitHub Stars、项目案例，而非空洞自我吹嘘 |
| **时机精准** | 项目刚融资、刚发版本、刚出事（需要修复）时联系命中率高 |
| **CTA 清晰** | 要一个具体行动："能聊 15 分钟吗？" 而不是"期待回复" |

### 1.2 邮件结构（黄金模板）

```
Subject: [具体观察到的事实] + [价值主张]（<50字）

Hi [名字],

[开场：你在哪里看到他们 / 他们最近做了什么 — 1句]

[痛点/机会：行业共性问题，不直接说他们有问题 — 1-2句]

[你的方案：具体能帮什么，带来什么结果 — 2-3句]

[证据：1行GitHub/案例/数据]

[CTA：具体请求，附时间选项 — 1句]

Best,
[你的名字]
[你的平台/GitHub链接]
```

### 1.3 主题行技巧
- ✅ `Saw your zkSync audit need — here's a faster approach`
- ✅ `Your protocol just raised — you need this infra fix`
- ✅ `Saw the Base developer event — relevant tooling we built`
- ❌ `Partnership Opportunity` / `AI Development Services` / `We can help`

### 1.4 跟进节奏
- **Day 0:** 发送首封邮件
- **Day 3:** 跟进 #1（换 Subject，重新说）
- **Day 7:** 跟进 #2（「最后一条消息」语气）
- **Day 14:** LinkedIn/Twitter DM 替代（不做第4封邮件）

### 1.5 关键禁忌
- 不加任何附件（先建立信任）
- 不群发 CC/BCC
- 不使用跟踪像素（会被识别为垃圾邮件）
- 不在周末/节假日发（Web3 是全球的，注意时区）

## 四、外联邮件模板库

### 模板 A — 短版（80字，极简）
```
Subject: Your protocol's gas spike caught my eye

Hi [Name],

Noticed [Protocol] gas costs surged post-update. 
We built a tooling that cuts Solidity audit time by 60% — 
happy to share a quick demo.

Open to a 15-min call this week?

Best,
[Name]
```

### 模板 B — 中版（150字，关系建立型）
```
Subject: Relevant work we did for [Similar Protocol]

Hi [Name],

[Project] just hit my radar — impressive TVL growth and 
the recent [specific event] shows the team is serious about scaling.

We're a small dev team specializing in [specific skill: 
ZKP circuits / Solidity security / Rust chain indexing] 
and we've helped protocols like [comparable project] 
cut their [specific metric] by [X%].

I put together a short case study here: [link]

Would a 20-min call to explore if we're a fit make sense?
I've got [Day] afternoon or [Day] morning your time.

Best,
[Name]
[GitHub / Platform link]
```

### 模板 C — 创始人直推版
```
Subject: Built something for [Big Name] — relevant for [Project] too

Hi [Founder Name],

I know you're heads down on [current priority]. 
We just wrapped up [specific deliverable] for [their competitor] 
and the approach could cut your [specific bottleneck] in half.

No pitch deck — just a 10-min call if you think it makes sense.

Interested?

[Name]
[1-line credibility]
```

### 模板 D — 危机切入版（安全事件后）
```
Subject: Quick audit flag for [Protocol] — can help

Hi [Name],

Saw the [incident description] on Rekt. 
We specialize in [specific vulnerability type] and have 
open capacity to run a full review this week.

Happy to share our methodology first — no commitment needed.

[Name]
[GitHub]
```
